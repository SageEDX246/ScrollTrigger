About me

The Wanderer came into existence in place of his previous incarnation after the latter expunged his previous appellations and their respective histories from Irminsul.

Personality

In several instances throughout the series, Wanderer is shown to prioritize independence and personal growth above all else, including his own safety and comfort. He is not afraid to take risks and try new things, and always approaches situations with a positive and optimistic outlook.
